About

A hybrid background in computer science and industrial design.

My work connects technical systems with human experience. I’m interested in HCI, AI-assisted workflows, robotics, and creative hardware products that make abstract digital behavior visible and discussable.

Before focusing on computer science, I studied industrial design, which shaped how I think about form, material, interaction, and user context. Now I use that design background to build more thoughtful technical projects.
